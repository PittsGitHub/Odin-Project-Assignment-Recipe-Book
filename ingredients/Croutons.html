Croutons

Description

Homemade Croutons

Ingredients

  • Day-old bread
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  • Cut bread into cubes.
  • Add your bread cubes to a baking sheet
  • Coat them in a mixture of ¼ cup melted butter, ¼ cup olive oil, two cloves of minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Toss the bread to evenly coat each cube
  • Bake for 15 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
