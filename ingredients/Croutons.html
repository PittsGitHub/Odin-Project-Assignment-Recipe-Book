Caesar Salad

Croutons

Description

Homemade Croutons

Ingredients

  • Day-old bread
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut bread into cubes.
  2. Add your bread cubes to a baking sheet
  3. Coat them in a mixture of ¼ cup melted butter, ¼ cup olive oil, two cloves of minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss the bread to evenly coat each cube
  5. Bake for 15 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
